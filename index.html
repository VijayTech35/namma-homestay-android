Namma HomeStay

Rural Stays, Real Experiences

v1.0.0 — Android

About

Namma HomeStay connects travelers with authentic rural homestays across Karnataka. Browse verified properties, book your stay, and experience the warmth of rural India — all from your phone.

Features

🔍 Browse & Search

Discover homestays with filters, search, and verified listings.

⭐ Reviews & Ratings

Read genuine guest reviews and rate your experience.

📅 Book Instantly

Check-in/out dates, guest count — book directly from the app.

❤️ Favorites

Save your favorite homestays for quick access.

🍽️ Daily Menu

View today's meals — AI-generated suggestions for hosts.

🗺️ Nearby Places

Explore trekking spots, temples, waterfalls around your stay.

🌙 Dark Mode

Comfortable viewing day or night.

🌐 Multi-Language

English, Hindi, and Kannada support.
